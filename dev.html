This project illustrates how a strategic combination of 3D modeling, AR integration, and
web development can elevate the promotion of a product, depends user engagement and interest.
The target for this is to show a dynamic digitalperformance that effectively communicated the
unique features of the AirPods to a wide audience.

The model of airpods and its charging case were made in cinema 4d software to enhance its publicity on social media.
